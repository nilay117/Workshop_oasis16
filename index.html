Fashion Photography

A one day workshop in Fashion Photography gives you a complete idea of camera settings and the settings to control lights with hands-on practice to take creative and innovative photographs. And also you will get a live shoot on a model.This intensive photography workshop will teach you all you need to know when it comes to fashion photography and studio lighting.

  • Familiarize yourself with studio lights
  • History of Fashion Photography.
  • Working With models (Male & Female)
  • Know about brands Like Vogue, Zara, UCB etc.
  • Overview of light diffusers, soft-boxes and other equipment used in the studio.
  • The differences between hard and soft light
  • Preparation of camera and lenses
  • Camera modes, white balance, aperture and shutter speed
  • Filters in the studio
  • Shallow depth of field and studio lights
  • Practical use, and various ways of lighting on different backgrounds
  • Lighting tricks

We welcome students with or without a DSLR both.
